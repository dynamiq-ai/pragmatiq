# Code of Conduct

> pragmatiq is an independent implementation inspired by the PRAGMA paper
> (arXiv 2604.08649) and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Revolut.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in pragmatiq welcoming, respectful, and
constructive for contributors and users regardless of background, identity,
experience level, or affiliation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Keep technical debate focused on evidence, reproducibility, and project goals.
- Avoid sharing private customer data, credentials, or proprietary banking
  records in issues, pull requests, discussions, or examples.
- Credit prior work clearly, including the PRAGMA paper when discussing the
  methods that inspired this implementation.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Misrepresenting pragmatiq as affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or
  officially connected to Revolut.
- Uploading copyrighted third-party assets, Revolut trademarks, Revolut trade
  dress, or copied paper figures without permission and attribution.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to `support@getdynamiq.ai`. Maintainers may remove content, close
threads, restrict participation, or take other reasonable action to protect the
project and its community.
